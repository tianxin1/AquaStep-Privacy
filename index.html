Privacy Policy for AquaStep

Last Updated: March 16, 2026

1. Overview

AquaStep ("the App") is a minimalist pedometer tool. We value your privacy and do not collect, store, or share any personal information.

2. HealthKit Data

AquaStep requests access to Apple's HealthKit (Step count) solely to drive the 3D underwater visual experience. This data is processed in real-time on your device and is never uploaded to any servers or shared with third parties.

3. No Ads & No Tracking

The App does not use any advertising SDKs or third-party tracking tools. Your activity remains private.
